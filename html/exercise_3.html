Grilled Cheese

Ingredients

2 Tablespoons unsalted butter

2 Tablespoons currant or fig jam

2 slices Cheddar

2 slices cooked bacon

1/4 small pear, thinly sliced


Instructions

1. Melt the butted in a small skillet over medium-low heat. Spread the jam on 1 slice of the bread and form a sandwich with the cheese, bacon, and pear.

2. Cook the sandwich, covered, until the bread is toasted and the cheese is melted, 2 to 3 mins per side.
